NEXT STEPS TO ENABLE SMTP
=========================
To enable actual email sending via Office 365, do ONE of the following:

OPTION 1: Generate App-Specific Password (Recommended)
- Go to https://account.microsoft.com/account/manage-my-microsoft-account
- Navigate to Security settings
- Create an App-Specific Password
- Use the new password in .env MAIL_PASSWORD field

OPTION 2: Disable Security Defaults (Less Secure)
- Contact your Office 365 administrator
- Have them disable "Security defaults" in Azure AD
- Requires organization approval

OPTION 3: Use Alternative Email Provider
- Switch to another SMTP provider (SendGrid, Mailgun, Postmark, etc.)
- Update .env MAIL_MAILER, MAIL_HOST, MAIL_PORT, MAIL_USERNAME, MAIL_PASSWORD
